About

A fun and friendly kitty group for working moms. We plan monthly contributions, events, and interactive activities to stay connected and empowered. Together, we share experiences, celebrate milestones, and support each other through both challenges and achievements. From fun get-togethers to meaningful discussions, this group is all about building lasting bonds, financial discipline, and women’s empowerment in a safe and positive space. Join us to learn, grow, and create joyful memories with like-minded women.
